Criteria & Rules

Registration

  • Available Online via Google Form (link in registration section).
  • On-site registration is also available.
  • Can register via QR code on the day of the event.

Participation

The symposium is open to all UG & PG students from all departments. Participants are encouraged to leverage their interdisciplinary skills.

Judging

All events will be judged by an esteemed panel consisting of Professors based on innovation, execution, and presentation.

Prizes & Rewards

  • Certificates of participation/merit will be provided.
  • Special Mementos for top performers.

Team Size

Teams must adhere to the limits: Minimum 1 – Maximum 2 members (this depends on the specific event rules).
